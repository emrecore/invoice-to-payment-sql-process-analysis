# KPI Definitions

## Overview

This document defines the key performance indicators used to evaluate the Invoice-to-Payment process.

The KPIs focus on invoice workload, payment performance, process efficiency, exception handling, vendor performance, department performance, and SLA compliance.

## Invoice Volume and Financial Exposure

### Total Invoices

Measures the total number of invoices included in the analysis.

```text
COUNT(invoice_id)
```

This KPI indicates overall invoice processing workload.

### Total Invoice Amount

Measures the total financial value of all invoices.

```text
SUM(invoice_amount)
```

This KPI indicates the financial volume processed through Accounts Payable.

### Average Invoice Amount

Measures the average financial value per invoice.

```text
AVG(invoice_amount)
```

This KPI provides context on the typical size of invoice transactions.

### Open Invoice Count

Measures the number of invoices that have not yet been paid.

```text
COUNT(invoices where payment_status = 'Open')
```

This KPI highlights unresolved payment cases.

### Open Invoice Amount

Measures the total value of invoices that remain unpaid.

```text
SUM(invoice_amount where payment_status = 'Open')
```

This KPI represents open financial exposure.

## Payment Performance

### Payment Timing

Classifies payments based on the internally scheduled payment date.

Possible categories are:

* Paid Early
* Paid On Time
* Paid Late
* Open

### Payment Delay Days

Measures the difference between the actual payment date and the scheduled payment date.

```text
DATEDIFF(actual_payment_date, scheduled_payment_date)
```

A positive result indicates a late payment.

### Late Payment Count

Measures the number of payments completed after the scheduled payment date.

```text
COUNT(actual_payment_date > scheduled_payment_date)
```

This KPI identifies payment execution issues.

### Late Payment Rate

Measures the share of payments completed late.

```text
Late Payments / Total Payments
```

This KPI indicates how reliably the payment schedule is followed.

### Average Late Payment Delay

Measures the average number of days late among delayed payments.

```text
AVG(actual_payment_date - scheduled_payment_date)
```

This KPI indicates the severity of payment delays.

## Process Efficiency

### Receipt-to-Payment-Scheduled Time

Measures the time from invoice receipt to payment scheduling.

```text
Payment Scheduled Timestamp - Invoice Received Timestamp
```

This KPI measures internal processing efficiency before payment execution.

### Invoice-to-Payment Cycle Time

Measures the time from invoice receipt until payment.

```text
Paid Timestamp - Invoice Received Timestamp
```

This KPI measures end-to-end duration for completed invoice cases.

### Open Case Age

Measures the elapsed time from invoice receipt to the latest available event for an unfinished invoice.

```text
Latest Event Timestamp - Invoice Received Timestamp
```

This KPI should be used for open cases instead of completed cycle time.

### Average Process Step Duration

Measures the average time between consecutive process events.

```text
AVG(Current Event Timestamp - Previous Event Timestamp)
```

This KPI identifies slow transitions within the workflow.

### Bottleneck Duration

Measures the average duration of a specific event transition.

Examples include:

* Data Validation to PO Matching
* PO Matching to Approval
* Approval to Payment Scheduled
* Payment Scheduled to Paid

This KPI identifies the slowest process steps.

## Exception Handling

### Exception Invoice Count

Measures the number of invoices assigned to an exception type.

```text
COUNT(exception_type_id IS NOT NULL)
```

This KPI indicates the volume of non-standard processing.

### Exception Rate

Measures the share of invoices that involve an exception.

```text
Exception Invoices / Total Invoices
```

A high exception rate can indicate manual effort, weak master data, or insufficient process standardization.

### Exception Resolution Time

Measures the time between an exception being raised and a correction-related event.

```text
Correction Timestamp - Exception Raised Timestamp
```

This KPI evaluates how efficiently exceptions are handled.

### Average Exception Resolution Time

Measures the average duration required to resolve a specific exception type.

```text
AVG(Exception Resolution Time)
```

This KPI helps prioritize exception categories that require process improvement.

### Exception Impact Amount

Measures the total invoice value associated with exception cases.

```text
SUM(invoice_amount for exception invoices)
```

This KPI helps distinguish frequent minor exceptions from financially significant ones.

## Vendor Performance

### Vendor Invoice Volume

Measures the number of invoices associated with a vendor.

```text
COUNT(invoice_id by vendor)
```

This KPI indicates operational workload generated by a vendor.

### Vendor Invoice Amount

Measures the total invoice value associated with a vendor.

```text
SUM(invoice_amount by vendor)
```

This KPI indicates the financial importance of a vendor relationship.

### Vendor Exception Rate

Measures the share of a vendor's invoices that involve exceptions.

```text
Vendor Exception Invoices / Vendor Total Invoices
```

This KPI identifies vendors associated with recurring process problems.

### Vendor Open Invoice Amount

Measures the total unpaid invoice amount associated with a vendor.

```text
SUM(open invoice amount by vendor)
```

This KPI identifies vendor-related financial exposure.

### Vendor Average Cycle Time

Measures the average completed invoice-to-payment duration by vendor.

```text
AVG(invoice-to-payment cycle time by vendor)
```

This KPI identifies vendors linked to slower processing flows.

### Vendor Performance Category

Classifies vendors based on exception frequency, open invoice exposure, payment performance, and process duration.

Possible categories are:

* Reliable
* Needs Monitoring
* Critical

## Department Performance

### Department Invoice Volume

Measures the number of invoices owned by a department.

```text
COUNT(invoice_id by department)
```

This KPI indicates invoice workload by department.

### Department Event Volume

Measures the number of process events handled by a department.

```text
COUNT(event_id by department)
```

This KPI indicates operational activity within the workflow.

### Average Department Step Duration

Measures the average duration of process steps assigned to a department.

```text
AVG(step duration by department)
```

This KPI identifies departments associated with longer waiting times.

### Approval Waiting Time

Measures the average time between the previous process event and the approval event.

```text
AVG(Approval Timestamp - Previous Event Timestamp)
```

This KPI evaluates approval efficiency.

### Open Invoice Exposure by Department

Measures the total unpaid invoice value owned by a department.

```text
SUM(open invoice amount by department)
```

This KPI helps prioritize follow-up actions.

## SLA Performance

### Processing SLA

The project defines an internal processing target:

```text
Payment Scheduled within seven days after Invoice Received
```

### SLA Compliance

Measures the share of invoices scheduled for payment within the seven-day target.

```text
Invoices Within SLA / All Invoices
```

### SLA Breach Count

Measures the number of invoices scheduled for payment after the SLA threshold.

```text
COUNT(receipt-to-payment-scheduled time > 7 days)
```

### Not Scheduled Yet Count

Measures the number of invoices without a payment scheduling event.

```text
COUNT(payment scheduled timestamp IS NULL)
```

This KPI highlights unresolved process cases.

## Process Variants

### Process Variant

Represents the ordered event sequence of an invoice.

```text
GROUP_CONCAT(event_name ordered by event_timestamp)
```

This KPI makes standard and non-standard process paths visible.

### Variant Frequency

Measures how many invoices follow the same process sequence.

```text
COUNT(invoices per process variant)
```

This KPI identifies dominant workflows and unusual process patterns.

### Variant Cycle Time

Measures the average cycle time of invoices following a specific process variant.

```text
AVG(cycle time by process variant)
```

This KPI identifies inefficient process paths.

## Interpretation Notes

* Payment timing is measured against the internal scheduled payment date, not the contractual vendor due date.
* Completed cycle time is only meaningful for invoices with a recorded `Paid` event.
* Open cases should be assessed through open case age.
* Process step duration is attributed to the department responsible for the current event.
* Purchase order and invoice differences are treated as review cases rather than automatic errors.
* SLA thresholds and vendor categories are illustrative because the dataset is synthetic.
